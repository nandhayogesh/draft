About A1 Physio And Rehab - Home center

At A1 Physio And Rehab Center, we're dedicated to providing high-quality, patient-centred physiotherapy care in the comfort of our patients' own homes. Our team of experienced physiotherapists are passionate about helping individuals achieve optimal physical function, mobility, and independence. We believe in providing compassionate, expert care that's tailored to each patient's unique needs and goals. With a focus on convenience, collaboration, and evidence-based practices, we're committed to helping our patients improve their overall quality of life. Whether you're recovering from an injury, managing a chronic condition, or simply looking to improve your physical function, we're here to help. Contact us today to learn more about our home care physiotherapy services and how we can support you on your journey to better health.

Our Mission

Our A1 physio and rehab center strive hard to provide high quality physical rehabilitation services, our goal is to restore the functional of our patients back to their optimum level by incorporate effective and evidence base practice and treat them with companion and like family.

Our Vision

We strive to creative a place where patients receive outstanding and personalized care that inspires health and happiness.

Our Values

"At A1 Physio And Rehab Center, our values are built on compassion, expertise, and convenience. We prioritize our patients' well-being, providing personalized care with empathy and understanding. Manual Therapy

  • KCAT Theraphy

Excersise Therapy

Exercise is the best therapy for a healthy body and mind:

  • Strengthening Exercises
  • Aerophic Excersise
  • Resistence Band Excersise

Instrumental Therapy

  • IASTM (Instrument Assisted Soft Tissue Mobilization)

Hydro Theraphy

Cupping Theraphy

Needling Theraphy

Tapping Therapy
